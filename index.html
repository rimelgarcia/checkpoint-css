About Us

Our Story

Established in 2022, Dar El Jasmine began as a passionate tribute to Tunisia's rich culinary heritage. After years of training in both traditional Tunisian kitchens and modern European restaurants, Chef Malik returned to his homeland with a vision: to honor traditional flavors while embracing contemporary techniques. Starting with family recipes passed down through generations, our menu transforms classic dishes like seafood couscous and brik à l'oeuf into elegant, refined experiences. Located in a beautifully renovated 19th-century building in downtown Tunis, our restaurant combines authentic Tunisian hospitality with modern sophistication. Every dish tells a story of our Mediterranean roots while looking confidently toward the future.

We believe that dining is more than just eating – it's an experience to be savored. Our warm, inviting atmosphere and attentive service create the perfect backdrop for memorable meals with friends and family.
